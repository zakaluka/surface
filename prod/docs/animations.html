Animations

Animations bring your page to life.

I fade in from the top
I fade in from the right with a 1.5s delay

Code

Direction

There are 4 classes for fading in.

  • .fade-in-from-top
  • .fade-in-from-bottom
  • .fade-in-from-left
  • .fade-in-from-right
				
<div class="fade-in-from-top">
I fade in from the top
</div>
<div class="fade-in-from-left">
I fade in from the right
</div>

Delays

You can delay an animation for up to 10 seconds.

Deprecated: The suffixes go up to 100. 5 is 0.5s, 25 is 2.5s and so on.

  • .anim-delay--5
  • .anim-delay--10
  • .anim-delay--15
  • .anim-delay--20

The suffixes now match the ms of delay. They start at 500ms (0.5s) and go up to 10,000ms (10s)

  • .anim-delay--500
  • .anim-delay--1000
  • .anim-delay--1500
  • .anim-delay--2000
  • ...
  • .anim-delay--10000
				
<div class="fade-in-from-top anim-delay--500">
I have 0.5s delay
</div>
<div class="fade-in-from-left anim-delay--5000">
I have a 5s delay
</div>
